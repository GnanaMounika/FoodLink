What we do

Welcome to GRUB COMM, a community-driven platform that aims to tackle two of the most pressing issues of our time: food waste and hunger. At GRUB COMM, we believe that no one should go hungry while good food is going to waste. We are passionate about creating a world where surplus food can find its way to those who need it, and where food waste is minimized, and we are committed to making it happen. Our platform is designed to make food donation and redistribution easy, efficient, and safe. Whether you are an individual, a business, or a community organization, you can use GRUB COMM to donate or claim food donations, and to connect with other members of the community who share your values and vision.
